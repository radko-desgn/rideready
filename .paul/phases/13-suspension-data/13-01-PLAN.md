<objective>
## Goal
Add four missing models to `suspensionData`: RockShox Boxxer (DH fork), RockShox Vivid (air shock), RockShox Vivid Coil, and Fox DHX2 (DH coil shock) — completing the DH/enduro coverage gap across both brands.

## Purpose
The Boxxer is the dominant DH fork worldwide and has no entry. The Vivid was a flagship enduro/DH shock for years and still runs on many bikes. Fox's coil DH shock (DHX2) is absent — Float DHX in the data is air-only. These omissions make the app useless for a large slice of riders with DH builds or older enduro builds.

## Output
- `suspensionData.brands.rockshox.forks`: Boxxer appended after Zeb
- `suspensionData.brands.rockshox.shocks`: Vivid + Vivid Coil appended after Super Deluxe Coil
- `suspensionData.brands.fox.shocks`: DHX2 appended after Float DHX
- All four models appear in the fork/shock dropdowns and calculate correctly
</objective>
