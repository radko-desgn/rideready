<objective>
## Goal
Add two new brands to the suspension calculator: Marzocchi (Bomber Z1, Z2 forks + Bomber CR coil shock) and Cane Creek (Helm fork + DB Coil IL shock) — expanding coverage to budget-accessible and premium segments not currently represented.

## Purpose
Marzocchi (Fox-owned budget brand) has a large installed base — Bomber Z1/Z2 are popular entry-level trail/enduro forks. Cane Creek is the leading premium independent brand; Helm fork and DB Coil are highly regarded among enduro/DH riders. Both brands are frequently searched and currently produce zero results in the app.

## Output
- `BRAND_LABELS` updated with `marzocchi: 'Marzocchi'` and `cane_creek: 'Cane Creek'`
- `suspensionData.brands.marzocchi` added with forks [Bomber Z1, Bomber Z2] and shocks [Bomber CR]
- `suspensionData.brands.cane_creek` added with forks [Helm] and shocks [DB Coil IL]
- Both brands appear in fork and shock dropdowns, grouped by brand label
</objective>
