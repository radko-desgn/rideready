<objective>
## Goal
Add target sag in mm to fork result cards and a "Verify your sag" collapsible guide to both fork and shock result cards.

## Purpose
Sag % is already shown as a chip but is only meaningful to experienced riders. Showing mm gives every rider a number they can actually measure with a ruler on the trail. The verify guide turns the calculator into a setup companion — riders stay in the app through the whole setup process, not just the pressure calc.

## Output
- Fork result card: sag chip upgraded to show `25% · 37–45mm` (% × travel_mm range)
- Shock result card: sag chip unchanged (% only — no stroke_mm data in model DB)
- Both cards: collapsible "Verify your sag" guide below damping section
</objective>
